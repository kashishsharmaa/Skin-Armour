Skin Armour is a Flask application that utilizes AI algorithms to detect skin diseases and provide treatment susceptibility for patients. It offers an intelligent system that analyzes patient data and provides valuable insights for effective disease diagnosis and treatment planning.


## Features

- Skin Disease Detection: Skin Armour leverages advanced AI algorithms to analyze patient symptoms and data to accurately detect various diseases.
- Treatment Susceptibility: Based on the detected disease, Skin Armour provides valuable information on treatment susceptibility, assisting healthcare professionals in making informed decisions.
- User-Friendly Interface: Skin Armour offers an intuitive and easy-to-use interface, making it accessible for both medical professionals and patients.
- Data Privacy and Security: Skin Armour prioritizes data privacy and security, ensuring that patient information is handled with the utmost confidentiality and adheres to industry standards.
  
Added Feature:
Dermatologist Finder:
- Integrated Google Maps API to suggest nearby dermatologists
- Used browser geolocation for real-time location tracking
